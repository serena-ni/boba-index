Boba Flavor Index

Boba (also known as bubble tea) is a tea-based drink originating in Taiwan, typically combining brewed tea, milk or fruit flavors, sweetness, and chewy tapioca pearls. This index compares popular boba drinks using consistent sensory measurements.
